Understanding Sjögren's

An autoimmune disorder characterized by dry eyes and dry mouth, often with systemic manifestations affecting multiple organs.

Key Features:

  • Persistent dry eyes and mouth
  • Dental caries and oral thrush
  • Fatigue and joint pain
  • Possible lung or kidney involvement
