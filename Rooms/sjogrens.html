About Sjogren's Syndrome

Sjogren’s Syndrome is an autoimmune disease in which the body’s immune system attacks its own moisture-producing glands. This primarily results in dry eyes and dry mouth, but it can also affect the skin, joints, and organs. Management involves symptom relief with artificial tears and saliva substitutes, along with medications to control immune activity when needed.
