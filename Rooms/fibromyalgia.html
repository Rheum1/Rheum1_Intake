About Fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia is a chronic condition characterized by widespread musculoskeletal pain, fatigue, and tenderness in localized areas. It often coexists with other conditions such as irritable bowel syndrome, depression, and anxiety. Management includes lifestyle adjustments, medications, and physical therapy.
