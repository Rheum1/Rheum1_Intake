Understanding Fibromyalgia

Fibromyalgia is a chronic condition characterized by widespread musculoskeletal pain accompanied by fatigue, sleep, memory and mood issues. Researchers believe that fibromyalgia amplifies painful sensations by affecting the way your brain processes pain signals.

Key Features of Fibromyalgia

  • Widespread pain
  • Fatigue
  • Cognitive difficulties ("fibro fog")
  • Sleep disturbances
  • Other symptoms like headaches, IBS, etc.
