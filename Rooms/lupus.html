Overview

Lupus is an autoimmune disease that can affect skin, joints, kidneys, and more.

Symptoms

  • Joint pain and swelling
  • Fatigue
  • Rash (especially across cheeks)
  • Mouth/nose sores

Early lab work can help clarify organ involvement and guide care.
