Understanding Lupus

Lupus is an autoimmune disease where the body's immune system attacks its own tissues, causing inflammation and tissue damage in multiple organ systems.

Key Features:

  • Butterfly-shaped facial rash
  • Joint pain and swelling
  • Photosensitivity
  • Kidney, heart, or lung involvement
