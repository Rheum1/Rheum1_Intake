Rheumatoid Arthritis (RA)

← Back to Home

Overview

RA is a chronic autoimmune disorder that affects joints, causing pain, stiffness, and swelling.

Symptoms

  • Morning joint stiffness
  • Symmetrical joint pain
  • Swelling in small joints (hands, wrists, feet)

Early treatment can prevent long-term joint damage — start with a symptom screen.
