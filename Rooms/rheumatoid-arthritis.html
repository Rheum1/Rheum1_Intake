Understanding RA

Rheumatoid arthritis is a chronic inflammatory disorder affecting joints, causing painful swelling that can lead to bone erosion and joint deformity.

Key Features:

  • Symmetrical joint swelling
  • Morning stiffness >1 hour
  • Progressive joint damage
  • Systemic symptoms possible
