Understanding Gout

Gout is a form of inflammatory arthritis caused by urate crystal deposition in joints, leading to sudden, severe pain attacks.

Key Features:

  • Sudden, severe joint pain (often 1st MTP)
  • Redness, swelling, and warmth
  • Recurrent attacks if untreated
  • Tophi formation in chronic cases
