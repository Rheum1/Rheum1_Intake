About Spondyloarthritis

Spondyloarthritis is a family of inflammatory rheumatic diseases that primarily affect the spine and, in some cases, the joints of the arms and legs. The hallmark feature is inflammation where ligaments and tendons attach to bones, known as enthesitis. This group includes conditions like ankylosing spondylitis and psoriatic arthritis. Common symptoms include back pain, stiffness, and fatigue. Treatment options include NSAIDs, physical therapy, and biologic medications to control inflammation and maintain mobility.
