Overview

A group of inflammatory conditions including ankylosing spondylitis and psoriatic arthritis.

Symptoms

  • Low back pain that improves with activity
  • Heel or foot pain
  • Joint stiffness
  • Family history of autoimmune disease

HLA-B27 testing and imaging may be part of your initial workup.
