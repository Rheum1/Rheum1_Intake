Understanding Spondyloarthritis

This group of inflammatory diseases affects the spine and sacroiliac joints, causing chronic back pain and stiffness that improves with movement.

Key Features:

  • Chronic back pain starting before age 40
  • Morning stiffness >30 minutes
  • Improvement with exercise
  • Possible peripheral joint involvement
