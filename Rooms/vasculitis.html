Overview

Vasculitis involves inflammation of blood vessels and can affect any organ system, requiring prompt diagnosis.

Symptoms

  • Unexplained fatigue or fever
  • Skin rashes or ulcers
  • Numbness or nerve issues
  • Kidney or lung involvement

Initial lab work may include CBC, ESR/CRP, and autoantibody panels.
