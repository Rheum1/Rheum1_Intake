Why Use This Site?

Rheum One helps patients prepare for their rheumatology appointment by completing a secure symptom survey. Based on your responses, we generate a personalized encounter note and a list of labs tailored to your needs — saving time and ensuring your concerns are clearly communicated. Once the survey is completed, please visit the Sterling center between the hours of 9am-3pm for your lab drawing.
