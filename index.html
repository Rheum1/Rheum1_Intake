Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need insurance to use this?

No. The symptom survey is available to all patients, regardless of insurance status. The $20 fee covers survey processing and document generation.

How is my data handled?

Your data is processed securely through Jotform (HIPAA Compliant) and stored privately within a HIPAA compliant ecosystem ‚Äî not on this site. We follow strict privacy protocols to protect your health information.

What if I already have labs from another provider?

You can bring them to your appointment, but our survey ensures we don't miss any important tests specific to rheumatologic conditions. The generated lab order will account for any recent testing.

Can I get a refund if I don't complete the survey?

The $20 fee is non-refundable as it covers the processing and document generation system. However, if you experience technical difficulties, please contact us for assistance.

How long does it take to get my lab order and encounter note?

Documents are typically generated within 48 hours of survey completion. You'll receive an email notification when they're ready.
